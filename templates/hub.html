Frequently Asked Questions

What is FixHub? +
FixHub is a community-driven platform where citizens can report local issues like potholes, broken streetlights, and garbage.
How do I report an issue? +
Simply submit a report with a description, images, and location using our interactive map.
Who can access and respond to reported issues? +
Verified government or official personnel can log in and update the status of issues based on ground action taken.
Can I upvote issues raised by others? +
Yes! If you see an issue that affects your neighborhood, you can upvote it once to help prioritize it.
