❓ Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to help clarify your doubts

What is FixHub? +
FixHub is a community-driven platform for reporting local civic issues like potholes, garbage, and streetlight failures.
How do I report an issue? +
First verify your ID, then fill in the issue details, select a location on the map, and submit.
Can I track my submitted issues? +
Yes, go to the “Track” section to see your issue’s current status and community upvotes.
Who updates the status of issues? +
Government officials or authorized personnel can log in and update the issue status directly.
Why do I need to verify identity? +
To prevent spam, fake issues, and ensure that only genuine citizens can report and upvote civic problems.
